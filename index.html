CERITFY YOUR BUILDING AS A DIGITAL TWIN TOKEN (DTT)

The Digital Twin Token is a unique digital asset backed by property data. Magma combines your building's components and system essential information into a DTT.

CONNECT THE BUILDING TWIN TO YOUR STAKEHOLDERS

Users connect directly to the DTT without any intermediaries. As Stakeholders , they have the power to upload and verify information that enriches the DigitalTwin Token. A Stakeholders ability to access validated-data to perform tasks depends on their specific role.

GROW THE VALUE OF YOUR DIGITAL TWIN TOKEN.
